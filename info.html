Information about this Kiosk

This Recycling Kiosk helps people of every kind have access to a safe and environmentally friendly way to recycle any sort of waste. Our Recycling Kiosks are also scattered in Makers Valley to ensure everyone there has access to these recycling opportunities for a better future!

Follow these steps to recycle your waste:

1 Print a tag. 2 Attach it to the bag containing your item for recycling. 3 Drop the item in the hatch.

Recycling Points

Recycling points are gained based on the value of the item recycled. Check on the LOCK Recycling Kiosk Website to view progress of item drop offs. Items can be redeemed at LOCK shops and other participating shops for clothing and other items.
